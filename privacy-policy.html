Privacy Policy

The use of ZEBEDEE Verifier ("Bot") within a server requires the collection of specific user/server data ("Data"). This Data includes, but is not limited to Discord user ID values, Discord server ID values, Discord role ID values, and information provided by ZEBEDEE LLC during OAuth2 authentication. Your use of the Bot implies agreement to the terms outlined in this Policy.

Access to Data

Access to Data is strictly limited to the developers of the Bot and is granted solely within the scope necessary for development, testing, and feature implementation purposes. Data is neither sold nor shared with any third party, except where mandated by law or a Terms of Service agreement. Upon request, you can review the data through @coffeel.arkoburger.

Storage of Data

Data is stored in an SQLite file hosted on Heroku alongside the Bot. While the database is safeguarded to prevent external access, we offer no guarantee against unintentional or malicious breaches. In the event of unauthorized access, affected users will be notified through the Discord client application.

User Rights

You reserve the right to request access to the Data associated with your Discord account at any time. To initiate such a request, please submit it through the Discord Server. Additionally, you have the right to request the removal of relevant Data.

Underage Users

The Bot is not intended for use by minors under the age of 13 or below the age of legal consent in their respective country, as stipulated in the Discord Terms of Service. No information will be knowingly stored from underage users. If it comes to our attention that a user is underage, all necessary steps will be taken to delete their stored data.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Please note that this Privacy Policy is subject to change without prior notice. It is your responsibility to review this policy periodically for any updates or revisions.
